10 tips to keep you safe during a riot

1. Avoid large crowds and avoid areas where a riot may be taking place.

2. If you must go into a potentially dangerous area, travel in a group and stay together.

3. Do not get involved in any physical confrontation - stay away from any violence.

4. If you see any signs of a riot, leave the area immediately.

5. Be aware of your surroundings and trust your instincts. If something feels wrong, leave the area.

6. Stay calm and avoid reacting to any provocations.

7. Have a first-aid kit with you in case you or someone else needs medical attention

8. Carry a charged cell phone with you and have a plan for getting in touch with your family or friends.

9. Have an emergency plan in place in case you get separated from your group.

10. Follow the directions given by local authorities and keep your distance from any areas where violence is taking place.
