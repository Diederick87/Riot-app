About Riot Watch

Riot Watch was created to safeguard people and share information about current events and riots , on this website you may find articles about previous strikes or riots and safety information this site may be updated from time to time to keep up to date with the latest riot news and articles as they are being received or shared

This site contains news snippets or articles from google and other websites about the riot events from the past as well as a riot map to display hot spots where riots have been and a few statistics will be placed as well to show estimated damage costs , how many people were involved estimated and the duration of the event and area affected by the riot , this site also has a riot safety page dedicated to give you some general tips and pointers on how to stay safe during a riot and a practical guide to get through the situation , some new features may be implemented later on.
