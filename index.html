Welcome to Riot Watch

In Southern Africa, strikes can erupt with little warning and can quickly descend into chaos and destruction of shops, buildings and infrastructure. As people take to the streets in protest, the motives behind their grievances can often remain unclear, as the situation rapidly spirals out of control. Property can be destroyed and tires can be burned as the strikes become increasingly violent, and innocent bystanders can find themselves in life-threatening danger. With the sheer mass of people involved, police and security forces may be unable to contain the situation as violence can escalate even faster sometimes, making it even more hazardous. It is important to remember the potential consequences of such demonstrations, and the need for caution when dealing with the situation..
